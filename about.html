About Us

Hi there! We're Ishaan and Sarang, two photography enthusiasts who started Photopix because we share a passion for capturing the world around us. Whether it's the beauty of nature, the energy of a bustling city, or the joy on someone's face, we believe that photos have the power to tell stories and evoke emotions.

Photopix is our way of sharing our love for photography with others. We hope this website inspires you to see the world through a new lens and maybe even pick up a camera yourself!

Our details:
We are Computer Engineers studying at at Pimpri Chinchwad Polytechnic, Akurdi.
Sarang is from Jalgaon, and Ishaan is from Pune. With our knowledge in web development and our passion in photography, we decided to design this website, so as to upgrade our skills.
